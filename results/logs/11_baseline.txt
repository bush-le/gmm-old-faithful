INTERPRETATION
======================================================================
Single Gaussian (K=1):
  - Expected to underfit: Old Faithful is bimodal, not unimodal.
  - The single Gaussian sits between the two clusters, assigning
    low probability to points in both modes.
  - Low log-likelihood confirms the need for K≥2 components.

K-Means:
  - Hard assignment: each point belongs to exactly one cluster.
  - Assumes spherical clusters with equal variance.
  - For Old Faithful's elliptical clusters, K-Means may misassign
    boundary points that a GMM would handle probabilistically.

GMM Advantage (to be compared after GMM fitting):
  - Soft assignment captures uncertainty for boundary points.
  - Full covariance models elliptical cluster shapes.
  - Higher log-likelihood expected due to better density estimation.
