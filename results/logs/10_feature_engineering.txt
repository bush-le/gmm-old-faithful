ENGINEERED FEATURES
------------------------------------------------------------

1. eruptions×waiting  (Interaction Feature)
   Formula:        eruptions × waiting
   Type:           Relationship / Interaction (§9.9)
   Justification:  Old Faithful's two eruption regimes exhibit
                   a strong positive correlation (r ≈ 0.90).
                   The product term captures the multiplicative
                   relationship: short eruptions pair with short
                   waits (product ~110), long eruptions pair with
                   long waits (product ~360). This creates a
                   single feature that encodes the regime identity.

2. eruptions²  (Polynomial Feature)
   Formula:        eruptions²
   Type:           Geometric / Polynomial (§9.9)
   Justification:  Eruption duration is bimodal (~2min vs ~4.5min).
                   Squaring amplifies the gap between modes:
                   2² = 4  vs  4.5² = 20.25 (5× separation vs 2.25×).
                   This makes cluster boundaries more distinct
                   for the GMM to detect.
